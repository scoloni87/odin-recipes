Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

Buffalo Chicken Dip is a very famous recipe championed by the greatest chefs in the world. It can be as spicy or mild as you'd like, but either way it'll leave you wanting more no matter how full you are.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Franks Red Hot Sauce
  • 2 cups canned shredded chicken
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1 package (8-ounces) cream cheese,softened

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Spook into shallow 1-quart baking dish.
  2. Bake 20 minutes or until mixture is headed through; stir. Sprinkle with green onions (optional), and serve with chips, crackers, and/or cut up veggies.
